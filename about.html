Beauty care with calm, clarity and confidence.

Lush Skin & Body is redesigned as a softer, clearer digital experience where users can understand services before committing to a booking.

Our approach

A calmer way to explore skin, body and beauty treatments.

This redesign focuses on making the treatment journey easier to follow. Instead of dense information and disconnected booking steps, the site guides users through service discovery, comparison, cart review and booking.

The visual style keeps Lush’s soft pink identity, but uses clearer spacing, stronger hierarchy, image-led sections and minimal navigation to create a more premium and readable experience.

What this redesign prioritises

Clarity

Clear categories and treatment cards help users understand options faster.

Control

A cart-based journey lets users review services before booking.

Calm

Minimal layouts and soft visuals reduce the feeling of being overwhelmed.
